Gyros

Description

Gyros are delicious Greek street food items. Most of the ingredients are easy to find. You will need to purchase pre-made Gyro meat though for this recipe.

Most of this recipe is just putting together the ingredients. After purchasing the meat and tzatziki, the rest is easy.

Ingredients

  • Pita Bread
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Tzatziki Sauce
  • Gyro meat or chicken
  • (Optional)Feta cheese

Steps

  1. Warm the pita bread in the microwave for 15 seconds
  2. Layer 6 slices of gyro meat on the pita
  3. Lay slices of onion and tomato to taste
  4. Cover with Tzatziki Sauce to taste
  5. (Optional)Sprinkle on Feta and Parsley if desired
